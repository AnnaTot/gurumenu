THE GURU

Hello! We're glad you're here! We would like to share with you the unique process of making our authentic Neapolitan pizzas.

Let us show you how our pizza doughs are made with special Neapolitan techniques and ingredients.

Our doughs are prepared using cold fermentation, a special method that guarantees perfect dough quality. During dough preparation, we first make a pre-dough, which is mixed with the main dough after resting.

We pay great attention to preserving the dough's structure by resting and laminating it multiple times to perfect the gluten network. Then we shape it into balls and let them rest again.

The pizza topped with original Italian ingredients is placed into the oven, where it bakes perfectly in just one and a half minutes. This is the moment when the crust turns crispy. We hope you enjoy the carefully crafted characteristics of our dough and the delicious pizza creations we offer. We look forward to welcoming you among our guests and hope you experience the special taste of freshly made pizzas!

A 10% service charge will be added for serving.

If you have any questions, we are at your disposal.
